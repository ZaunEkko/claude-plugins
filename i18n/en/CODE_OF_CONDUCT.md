# Code of Conduct

[简体中文](../../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [繁體中文](../zh-TW/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [日本語](../ja/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [한국어](../ko/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Our commitment

We are committed to an open, welcoming, and harassment-free collaboration environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Expected behavior

Constructive behavior includes respectful and empathetic communication, focusing on technical facts and verifiable impact, accepting feedback, correcting mistakes, respecting privacy and licensing, helping new contributors with context, and prioritizing the long-term interests of users and the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, threats, discrimination, persistent bad-faith communication, unwanted sexual attention, exposing private information or credentials, deliberate disruption of discussions or releases, organizing abuse or fraud, and other conduct clearly inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Scope and enforcement

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, reviews, release notes, and other spaces where someone represents this project. Maintainers may edit or remove violating content and may issue warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans according to severity.

For non-sensitive concerns, notify maintainers in the relevant thread. For confidential reports, use the repository's [private reporting channel](https://github.com/ZaunEkko/claude-plugins/security/advisories/new) with `Conduct report` in the title. Do not expose affected people's private information in a public issue.
